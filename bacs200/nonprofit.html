FRHS Games Society

Socialize with new people with similar interests!

Some games played

Smash Bros.

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is one of the major games that is played in Games Society.
There's multiple ways that people play the game, such as 8-person versus matches with random items, or competitive 2-person matches in a tournament style bracket.

Board games

Board games are widely played at Games Society due to their short length and ease to pick-up and play for new people. Many games played can have between 2 and 4 players, some even going up to as many as 8 or 10 people!

Meeting times

Games Society meets every Tuesday at lunch for the entire lunch period. You are able to do everything, as long as you leave time to clean up after yourself.

After school on Wednesdays, meetings are longer than the normal Tuesday meetings. Busses do not run this late, so keep in mind that you will need to arrange other transportation to get home.

Lock-Ins happen usually 4 times a year. These last for several hours and always take place on Fridays after school. Here you have more time to play games that last longer, like DND and some card games. At Lock-Ins there is also pizza able to be purchased.
