My Inspiration

To me, my inspiration is André 3000 from OutKast.


Who is he???

André 3000, famous musician known for his catchy choruses, smooth rhymes, and storytelling second to none, is my biggest inspiration.

He has released multiple albums under the group 'OutKast', comprised of him and Big Boi.

Not only are his songs amazing but he is a genuinely great person, being a vegan for almost 15 years and supporting several movements, such as BLM.
